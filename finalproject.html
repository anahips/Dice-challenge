Adult Conenital Heart Disease

How can we help?

Get in touch with the specialist nurse about your concern. We are available Monday-Friday from 9 am to 5 pm and will contact you within the day.

When to go to A&E or call 999: when you have sustained palpitations lasting for more than 15 minutes, chest pain worse on exertion, shortness of breath.
